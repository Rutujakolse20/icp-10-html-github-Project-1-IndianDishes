Heritage on a Plate: Gujarati Recipes

Khandvi-Recipe

         

"Click Here to see full recipe"

Soft,mushy,light and delightful.Khandvi
is one of most likeable Gujarati breakfast
recipes a person can have.Khandvi,among
the famous Gujarati dishes, gives an
irresistible sweet and salty taste with a
batter that consists of gram flour, salt,
and sugar. Another name given to it in
Marathi is ‘Suralichya vadya’ as it is
adorned by Gujarat is and Maharashtrians alike.

Dhokla-Recipe

         

"Click Here to see full recipe"

Arguably the most recognizable Gujarati food,
Dhoklais one of the most frequently consumed
Gujarati dishes in the world.Be it early morning,
late afternoon, or evening- it’s always the right
time for Gujarati cuisine lovers to eat Dhokla.
Spongy dish made of fermented rice & chickpeas
Dhokla is best consumed with Green Chutney
or Meethi Chutney (made of Dates and Tamarind)

Thepla-Recipe

         

"Click Here to see full recipe"

A very commonly consumed Gujarati food,Thepla is
flatbread prepared in multiple variations like with
fenugreek leaves, wheat flour, or cumin seeds.With
right combination of ingredientsin the Thepla dough,
the dish comes out with a vibrant flavour that is
nearly impossible to ignore.Combined with add-ons
like curds and chunda, Theplasare a popular portable
dish that can be consumed hot or cold while travelling.

Fafda-Jalebi-Recipe

         

"Click Here to see full recipe"

Gujarati cuisine is filled with a variety of snacks
& quick edible items. Fafda Jalebi is the tried &
tested,& sweet and salty Gujarati food that
available at every street corner in Gujarat.
Fafda is a crunchy snack that is made with gram
flour,turmeric,and carom seeds. A light snack
that is generally consumed anytime during the
day,is best enjoyed by Gujaratis with Jalebi
deep fried maida flour prepared in pretzel
or circular shapes.

Gujarati-Khichdi-Recipe

         

"Click Here to see full recipe"

Not very long ago, Khichdi was given the title
of the national food of India by the government.
A very common dish consumed in every part of
India, Gujarat also managed to create its own
version of Khichdi to cater to their taste buds.
Nutritious in content, and healthy in flavors
and taste, Gujarati Khichdi normally contains
ingredients like rice,cereals,vegetables& ghee.
Often consumed with Buttermilk, Khichdi is one
of the typical Gujarati dinner recipes.

Khakhra-Recipe

         

"Click Here to see full recipe"

The more people delve into Gujarati dishes,the
more they realize the importance and variety
of flatbreads in Gujarati cuisine. Khakhra,
another type of thin flat-bread, is a popular
Jain cuisine made of mat bean, wheat flour, and
oil. A common addition to the household Gujarati
breakfast recipes, Khakhra is the main food of
Gujarat and is a very nutritious snack best
enjoyed with spicy pickles or Meethi Chutney.
It is so delicious.
